Sticky Toffee Pudding Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g dates, pitted and chopped
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 150g brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line a baking dish.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the dates and baking soda. Add enough boiling water to cover the dates, and let them soak for about 5 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and brown sugar. Beat in the eggs one at a time.
  4. Mix in the flour, baking powder, and vanilla extract.
  5. Stir in the soaked dates, including the liquid.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and bake for about 30-40 minutes, or until a skewer comes out clean.
  7. Serve the sticky toffee pudding with your choice of toffee sauce or ice cream.
