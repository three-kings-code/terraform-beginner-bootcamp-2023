Dizzy Prince of the Yolkfolk Characters

Dizzy the Egg

The titular character, Dizzy is a kind-hearted and adventurous egg. He often finds himself in various perilous situations and must solve puzzles to overcome challenges in his quest to save his friends and family..

Daisy the Cow

Daisy is Dizzy's girlfriend, and she appears in several of the games. Dizzy frequently embarks on quests to rescue or help Daisy.

Dylan the Yolkfolk

Dylan is Dizzy's brother and is known for his bravery. He plays a significant role in various games, and his abilities complement Dizzy's.

Denzil the Yolkfolk

Denzil is another member of Dizzy's family, often portrayed as a clumsy character. Despite his clumsiness, he offers help and comedic moments throughout the series.

Grand Dizzy

Grand Dizzy is an elderly character in the Dizzy universe. He often provides guidance and wisdom to Dizzy during his adventures.

Wizard Theo

Wizard Theo is a friendly wizard who occasionally helps Dizzy with magical abilities and advice.

Zak the Magic Hatter

Zak is a magician and a recurring character. He often aids Dizzy with magical items and advice.

The Evil Wizard Zaks

Zaks is the primary antagonist in the Dizzy series. He is known for causing trouble and posing various threats that Dizzy must overcome

Pogie the Fluffle

Pogie is a friendly, furry creature that Dizzy befriends during his adventures. Pogie is known for his cheerful and cuddly nature. He often provides assistance by carrying items for Dizzy and helping him in various ways.

Tink the Mushroom

Tink is a quirky mushroom character who appears in some of the Dizzy games. Tink is known for his love of riddles and puzzles. He often challenges Dizzy with brain-teasers that must be solved to progress in the game.

Dozy the Dinosaur

Dozy is a lovable and somewhat slow-witted dinosaur character who appears in a few Dizzy games. Despite his simple nature, he plays an important role in helping Dizzy on his adventures.

The Troll

The Troll is a recurring enemy in the Dizzy series. Trolls are typically encountered in various locations and must be outsmarted or avoided by Dizzy to progress.

The Evil Dizzy Clones

In some Dizzy games, there are clones or doppelgangers of Dizzy created by the evil Wizard Zaks. These evil versions of Dizzy often cause trouble and add an extra layer of challenge to the gameplay.

Mimic the Parrot

Mimic is a chatty parrot that Dizzy encounters on some of his journeys. Mimic often offers clues, hints, and humorous commentary, adding to the fun and whimsy of the games.
