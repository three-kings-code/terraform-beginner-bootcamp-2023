Dizzy the Egg

The titular character, Dizzy is a kind-hearted and adventurous egg. He often finds himself in various perilous situations and must solve puzzles to overcome challenges in his quest to save his friends and family..

Daisy

Daisy is Dizzy's girlfriend, and she appears in several of the games. Dizzy frequently embarks on quests to rescue or help Daisy.

Dora

Dora is another friend of Dizzy who appears in various games. She sometimes finds herself in peril, requiring Dizzy's assistance.

Dylan

Dylan is Dizzy's brother and is known for his bravery. He plays a significant role in various games, and his abilities complement Dizzy's.

Denzil

Denzil is another member of Dizzy's family, often portrayed as a clumsy character. Despite his clumsiness, he offers help and comedic moments throughout the series.

Dozy

Dozy is a drowsy and sleepy character who occasionally needs Dizzy's help to wake up and get back to work.

Grand Dizzy

Grand Dizzy is an elderly character in the Dizzy universe. He often provides guidance and wisdom to Dizzy during his adventures.

Wizard Theo

Wizard Theo is a friendly wizard who occasionally helps Dizzy with magical abilities and advice.

The Evil Wizard Zaks

Zaks is the primary antagonist in the Dizzy series. He is known for causing trouble and posing various threats that Dizzy must overcome

Pogie the Fluffle

Pogie is a friendly, furry creature that Dizzy befriends during his adventures. Pogie is known for his cheerful and cuddly nature. He often provides assistance by carrying items for Dizzy and helping him in various ways.

Prince Clumsy

Prince Clumsy is a character who sometimes requires Dizzy's assistance, adding an element of rescue to the games.

Shmaus

Shmaus is a character who can provide important information or aid to Dizzy during his adventures.
